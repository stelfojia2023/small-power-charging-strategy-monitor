# Company Profile

## Current Business

- Supplies portable chargers and AC 3.5kW / 7kW chargers.
- Mainly serves automakers through ODM/OEM.
- Shipped around 700,000 portable chargers in 2025.
- Has major customer concentration in Wuling and XPeng.

## Strengths

- R&D capability.
- Quality control.
- Mass delivery.
- Existing automaker supply experience.

## Weaknesses

- Weak consumer brand.
- Limited channel power.
- Limited capital compared with large platform players.
- Limited installation service capability.

## Current Risks

- Negative gross margin risk in Wuling projects due to extremely low bidding from Zhida.
- Customer concentration in Wuling and XPeng.
- Portable charger and AC charger commoditization.
- Risk of entering a C-end brand/channel war without sufficient scarce resources.

## Strategic Goals

- Keep OEM/ODM as the base business.
- Expand from 2 major automaker customers to 5-8 customers.
- Build platform-based cost advantage through VAVE.
- Set negative-margin red lines.
- Avoid direct C-end brand war against Bull.
- Build automaker-same-source aftermarket products.
- Use regional installation partners instead of building a national installation network.
- Treat small-power DC as an order-driven pilot, not a full-line bet.
